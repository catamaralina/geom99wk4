京都市 | Kyoto

Place: Umekoji Park with Kyoto Aquarium in it.

Day: Day starting with Kyoto Botanical Gardens, then walking in Kamagawa Park along the river, a world protected area, and stopping to look at herons and other wildlife. All the while heading towards Kyoto National Garden to end the day.

Area: Arashiyama is a good place to wander and see what is interesting. Parks to walk in, monkeys to feed, things to see, food to eat.
